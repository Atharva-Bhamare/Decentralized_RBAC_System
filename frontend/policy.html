How Policy Management Works

1
Create Roles — Go to the Roles page and define hospital roles like Doctor, Nurse, or Pharmacist.
2
Create Permissions — Go to the Permissions page and define resource actions like "View Patient Records" or "Issue Prescription".
3
Assign Policy — Here, link a permission to a role on-chain via assignPermissionToRole().
4
Assign Staff — Go to the Staff page to assign a staff wallet to a role — they inherit all its permissions automatically.
5
Verify Access — Go to the Access Control page to verify any staff member's permissions on-chain at any time.
