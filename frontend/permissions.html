How Policies Work

1
Create Roles — Go to the Roles page and define hospital roles like Doctor, Nurse, Pharmacist.
2
Create Permissions — Define what actions can be performed on resources (done above).
3
Assign Policy — Link a permission to a role here. Stored permanently on-chain.
4
Assign Staff — Go to the Staff page to give a staff wallet a role carrying these permissions.
